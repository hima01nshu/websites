About Ricky Studio

At Ricky Studio, we believe in capturing the magic of your special moments with an artistic touch and heartfelt passion. Founded by Ricky, a seasoned photographer with over a decade of experience, our studio specializes in wedding photography that tells your unique story. We combine modern techniques with timeless style to deliver stunning photos that you’ll cherish forever.

Our team is dedicated to providing a personalized experience — from your first consultation to the final album delivery. We take pride in creating memories that last a lifetime.
